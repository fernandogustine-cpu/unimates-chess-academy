Uni-Mates Chess Academy Training Portal

The Uni-Mates Chess Academy Training Portal is a comprehensive online chess education platform designed to help students improve through structured learning, interactive training, and personalized coaching.

Built for coaches, schools, clubs, and competitive players, the platform combines lessons, puzzles, homework, progress tracking, and AI-assisted training into a single ecosystem.

Mission

Our mission is to provide world-class chess education through technology while maintaining the personal coaching philosophy of Coach Fernando and Uni-Mates Chess Academy.

We aim to help players of all ages develop strong chess fundamentals, tactical awareness, strategic understanding, and competitive confidence.
